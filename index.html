MyMoneyZen helps you organise your everyday money so that you can pay bills easily, fully enjoy spending and achieve your dreams sooner.

Proactive

We don't get you to track expenses because it's time consuming & just helps you track the mistakes you've already made. What we do, is make it easy for you to find out exactly how much of your pay is needed for your bills & financial commitments ahead of time - for reals (normally a very tricky number), and that’s when the magic happens. You can then harness the remainder by choosing ahead of time what you want to do with it; spend or save, or both.

Set and Forget

Follow through with your new budget is automatic, using a series of separate bank accounts & automatic transfers.

  1. Bills money is put aside ready to go
  2. Spending money hits your account once a week (see at a glance how much you have to spend, if there's money in your spending account, go nuts!)
  3. Savings goals are automatically being achieved

MyMoneyZen also keeps an eye on your bills bank balance for you, just in case, and lets you know if there’s a problem and how to rectify it, before there’s even a problem.

Education & Support

MyMoneyZen is a holistic approach. You're provided with everything you need to take control of your money and revolutionise this part of your life. Education, a powerful tool and coaching videos, to support you with the uncomfortable side of money, so you can get set up and start kicking goals.
